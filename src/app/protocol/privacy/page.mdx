# Privacy Policy

## 1. Information We Collect

- Email, login credentials, social login (Google, Apple, etc.)
- Generation history and product order data
- Shipping addresses and payment confirmation from third-party providers

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- To fulfill orders and generate custom designs
- To improve service experience and security
- To comply with legal requirements

## 3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information when:

- Required by law or legal process
- Necessary for payment, printing, or shipping providers
- Preventing fraud or abuse of the platform

## 4. Data Security

We use modern encryption and security practices to protect your personal information from unauthorized access.

## 5. Your Rights

You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data.
