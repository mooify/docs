# Refund Policy

## 1. General Policy

Because all items are custom-made based on your input or uploaded design, we **do not accept returns or issue refunds** except in cases of:

- Manufacturing defects
- Wrong item shipped
- Severe damage during delivery

## 2. Non-Refundable Situations

- You changed your mind after placing the order
- You are dissatisfied with the design, color, or style
- You entered an incorrect shipping address
- Misuse of the platform or misunderstood generation process

## 3. Refund Process

- Contact support within **72 hours** of delivery
- Provide photo evidence of the issue
- If approved, we will process a reprint or refund within **5 business days**
